Vasylivska Rotunda is a distinctive monument of Volyn sacral architecture of the end of the 12th century in the city Volodymyr in the area of ​​Vasylivshchyna on the outskirts of Zareche. The original building of the temple was different from the current one, it was a mixture of the local architectural tradition with elements of Gothic and Romanesque style The current outline is from the beginning of the 20th century. Belongs to examples of temple-building activity of Prince Roman Mstislavich.

Vasylkiv Carpathians
